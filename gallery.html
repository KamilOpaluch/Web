Survival-Hazard curves for Tesla Stock analyses

Kaplan-Meier survival curves and cumulative hazard functions for three scenarios:
1) 10% drop in stock price,
 2) R_alpha state (daily returns >= 1%),
 3) D_beta state (daily returns <= -1%).
 These charts provide insights into the Tesla stock's behavior over time, highlighting its volatility, potential for positive gains, and risk of significant losses.
Data scope: start='2010-01-01', end='2023-01-01'
